Apple Inc. - Q3 FY2024 Earnings Summary
Financial Services Division Research Report

COMPANY OVERVIEW
Apple Inc. (AAPL) reported strong third-quarter fiscal 2024 results, demonstrating resilience 
in its core product and services segments despite macroeconomic headwinds.

KEY FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS
- Total Net Revenue: $85.8 billion (up 5% year-over-year)
- Net Income: $21.4 billion (EPS: $1.40, up 11% YoY)
- Gross Margin: 46.3% (highest in company history for Q3)
- Operating Cash Flow: $29.1 billion
- Share Buybacks: $26 billion returned to shareholders

SEGMENT PERFORMANCE
iPhone Revenue: $39.3 billion (+3% YoY)
- iPhone 15 series showed continued strong demand globally
- India and Southeast Asia markets showed exceptional growth (>20%)
- Average Selling Price (ASP) increased to $942 from $918 in prior year

Mac Revenue: $7.0 billion (+2% YoY)
- M3 chip adoption driving upgrade cycle
- Professional and enterprise segment growing at 15% annually

iPad Revenue: $7.2 billion (+24% YoY)
- iPad Pro with M4 chip exceeded shipment forecasts
- Education segment contributed 31% of iPad revenue

Services Revenue: $24.2 billion (+14% YoY)
- App Store, iCloud, Apple Music, Apple TV+, Apple Pay
- Over 1 billion paid subscriptions globally (milestone)
- Services gross margin reached 74.1%

Wearables, Home and Accessories: $8.1 billion (-9% YoY)
- Apple Watch Series 9 tracking behind Series 8 launch pace
- AirPods category facing competitive pressure

GEOGRAPHIC PERFORMANCE
Americas: $37.7 billion (+6% YoY)
Europe: $21.8 billion (+7% YoY)
Greater China: $14.7 billion (-6% YoY) — regulatory headwinds persist
Japan: $4.8 billion (+4% YoY)
Rest of Asia Pacific: $6.8 billion (+11% YoY)

BALANCE SHEET HIGHLIGHTS
Total Cash and Equivalents: $153.0 billion
Total Debt: $101.0 billion
Net Cash Position: $52.0 billion
Shareholders' Equity: $56.0 billion

FORWARD GUIDANCE (Q4 FY2024)
- Revenue: $89.0-$91.0 billion (consensus: $89.9 billion)
- Gross Margin: 46.5%-47.0%
- Operating Expenses: $14.2-$14.4 billion
- Management noted:
  "We continue to see strong customer engagement across our ecosystem.
  The launch of Apple Intelligence in iOS 18 represents our largest
  software investment in company history."

RISK FACTORS
1. Regulatory Risk: Ongoing EU investigations into App Store practices
   (Digital Markets Act compliance costs estimated $1.5-2.0 billion)
2. China Market: Geopolitical tensions affecting brand perception
3. Competition: Samsung Galaxy and Pixel gaining share in emerging markets
4. Supply Chain: TSMC concentration risk for advanced node production
5. Macro: Consumer discretionary spending sensitivity

ANALYST COMMENTARY
Goldman Sachs (BUY, $225 PT): "Services margin expansion is structural.
Apple Intelligence will drive the next upgrade supercycle."

Morgan Stanley (OW, $230 PT): "India growth trajectory resembles China 
circa 2016. We model 40M units annually by FY2027."

JPMorgan (OW, $210 PT): "Valuation premium justified by installed base 
stickiness and services monetization flywheel."

CAPITAL ALLOCATION
Dividend per share: $0.25 (yield: ~0.5%)
Share repurchase authorization: $110 billion (ongoing)
Remaining authorization: $84 billion as of report date
Total capital returned to investors in last 5 years: $485 billion
